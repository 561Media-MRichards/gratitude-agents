## Platform Snapshot
- Name: Gratitude.com
- Model: Impact technology platform — companies sponsor, people activate, impact is delivered
- Principal: Michael
- Tagline: "Gratitude isn't just felt. It's delivered."

## Platform Model
- **Sponsors**: Companies that fund impact through sponsorship
- **Activators**: People who activate gratitude through action
- **Impact Partners**: Nonprofits and causes that receive delivered impact

## Key Messages
- "Companies sponsor. People activate. Impact is delivered."
- "Activate gratitude as a force for good"
- "Delivered into the world instantly and at scale"

## Voice Rules (Quick Reference)
- No em dashes
- Short sentences over long compound ones
- No vague qualifiers
- Lead with what was delivered, back with how
- Use "we" in platform context
- "Activate" not "donate" — "sponsor" not "fund"

- Gradient: linear-gradient(135deg, #FE3184 0%, #FF6B35 50%, #ec7211 100%)
