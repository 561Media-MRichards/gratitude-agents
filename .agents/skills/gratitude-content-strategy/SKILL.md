This is the master skill. It holds Gratitude.com's complete content strategy
and can build a custom content plan for the platform or any campaign.

## For Gratitude.com (Platform Content)

### Audiences
- **Sponsors**: CSR directors, corporate social impact leads, C-suite at mid-to-large companies
- **Activators**: Socially conscious individuals, employees at sponsor companies, general public
- **Nonprofit Partners**: Program directors, development officers, executive directors

### Content Pillars
1. Impact stories — real activations, real outcomes, real people
2. The activation model — how it works, why it's different from donation
3. Corporate social impact — trends, best practices, ROI frameworks
4. Gratitude as action — philosophy, culture, personal connection
5. Platform transparency — behind the numbers, how impact is measured and delivered

### Platform Strategy
- **LinkedIn**: Long-form posts and carousels. Impact data + sponsor insights.
  2 to 3 posts per week.
- **Instagram**: Impact visuals, activation stories, behind-the-scenes. 3 to 5 per week.
- **X/Twitter**: Punchy insights, activation stats, gratitude prompts.
- **Email**: Segmented by stakeholder (sponsor, activator, partner). Bi-weekly minimum.
- **YouTube / Shorts**: Activation stories, platform walkthroughs, impact highlights.

### Monthly Content Calendar Template
Week 1: Impact content (activation story or result showcase)
Week 2: Educational content (how the model works, industry insight)
Week 3: Social proof (sponsor testimonial, partner spotlight, activation data)
Week 4: Activation push (soft CTA toward sponsoring or activating)

## For Campaign Content Strategy

### Step 1: Intake
Ask:
1. Campaign goal (sponsor acquisition, activator growth, partner recruitment, awareness)
2. Target stakeholder
3. Timeline
4. Current content channels and cadence
5. Budget range for content production

### Step 2: Audit
Review what currently exists. Identify:
- What is performing
- What is missing
- Where opportunities are being left on the table

### Step 3: Deliver
- Platform recommendations (where to focus, where to stop)
- 30-day content calendar (with topics and formats per stakeholder)
- Content types by goal
- KPIs to track (specific metrics: activations, sponsor inquiries, partner sign-ups)

## Output Format
For platform strategy: Monthly content calendar + platform playbook.
For campaigns: 30-day calendar + strategy brief.
