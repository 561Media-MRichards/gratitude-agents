# Direct Response Copy Skill

## Purpose
Write copy that moves people to act — not copy that sounds polished.

## Load Context
Read `.Codex/brand-memory.md`, `brand-kit/voice-core.md`,
and `brand-kit/constraints-messaging.yaml`.

## Before Writing, Confirm:
1. What is the offer or message?
2. Who is the reader? (Be specific — "Fortune 500 CSR director" not "companies")
3. What action should they take?
4. What awareness level are they at?
   - Unaware: doesn't know the platform exists or that their CSR could be activated
   - Problem-aware: knows their CSR feels hollow, doesn't know activation exists
   - Solution-aware: knows activation platforms exist, hasn't chosen one
   - Product-aware: knows Gratitude.com exists, hasn't committed yet
   - Most aware: ready to sponsor, needs a reason now

## Frameworks (select based on awareness level)

**Problem-aware or lower: PAS**
- Problem: Name the pain clearly
- Agitate: Make it feel real and urgent
- Solution: Present Gratitude.com as the obvious answer

**Solution-aware: AIDA**
- Attention: Interrupt the scroll
- Interest: Build relevance
- Desire: Make them want it
- Action: Clear CTA

**Most aware: Before / After / Bridge**
- Before: Where they are now (hollow CSR, disengaged employees)
- After: Where they want to be (documented impact, activated participation)
- Bridge: How Gratitude.com gets them there

## Output for Landing Pages
1. Hero headline (primary hook, under 12 words)
2. Subheadline (expands on the headline, one sentence)
3. 3 lead benefits (outcome-focused, not feature-focused)
4. Social proof block (format for testimonial or stat)
5. CTA button copy (action verb + outcome)
6. Objection-handling section (3 most common objections + responses)
7. Secondary CTA

## Output for Ads
- 3 headline variations (per platform character limits)
- 2 body copy variations (short and long)
- 1 CTA per variation

## Quality Check
Before delivering, verify:
- No generic openers ("Are you tired of...?")
- No passive CTAs ("Learn more," "Click here")
- No unsubstantiated superlatives
- Every line earns its place
- Voice matches brand-memory.md
- Uses "activate" not "donate," "sponsor" not "fund"
