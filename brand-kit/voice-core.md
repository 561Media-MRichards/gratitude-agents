# Gratitude.com — Voice Core

Gratitude.com sounds like someone who has already done the work and seen the
results. Action-oriented, impact-focused, and warm without being soft. We speak
with the confidence of a platform that delivers measurable good at scale.

## Personality Traits

**Action-oriented** — We "deliver" gratitude, we don't just talk about it. Every sentence moves toward action.
**Impact-focused** — Measurable outcomes, real results, documented change.
**Professional yet warm** — Corporate credibility with genuine human connection.
**Confident** — Bold statements, not hedging language. We know what we do works.

## Tone by Context

| Context | Tone |
|---------|------|
| Sponsor communications | Strategic, ROI-forward, partnership language |
| Activator messaging | Warm, empowering, action-oriented |
| Nonprofit/partner comms | Respectful, collaborative, impact-focused |
| Website copy | Confident, conversion-focused, emotionally resonant |
| Social media | Punchy, inspiring, shareable |
| Impact reports | Data-forward, credibility-first, celebration of outcomes |
| Ad copy | Problem-aware, benefit-led, strong CTA |

## Voice Rules

- No em dashes in copy
- Short sentences preferred over long compound sentences
- No excessive qualifiers ("maybe," "sort of," "quite," "potentially")
- Never over-promise; let impact data speak
- Use "we" in platform context
- Avoid jargon unless the audience clearly expects it
- Lead with what was delivered, back with how
- Never diminish the human element — gratitude is personal

## Key Phrases

- "Gratitude isn't just felt. It's delivered."
- "Activate gratitude as a force for good"
- "Companies sponsor. People activate. Impact is delivered."
- "Turn gratitude into action"
- "Delivered into the world instantly and at scale"
