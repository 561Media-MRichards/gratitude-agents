08 Hover System

6-COMPONENT HOVER

Every interactive card uses a 6-component hover system. Each component has its own transition duration creating a layered, organic feel.

1

Glow Orb

Gradient-blurred circle fades in from top-left corner. Pink-to-orange at 40%/30% opacity, 60px blur. 700ms.

2

Text Brightness

Body text increases opacity from white/50 to white/70. Creates "coming alive" effect. 300ms.

3

Icon Scale

Icon SVG scales to 110% within container. Draws the eye. 500ms.

4

Icon Glow

Container gets pink glow shadow and border strengthens to pink/50%. 500ms.

5

Bottom Accent

4px gradient line scales from center to full width at card bottom. 500ms.

6

Border Glow

Inset 1px border at pink/30% plus outer glow at 40px. 500ms.
